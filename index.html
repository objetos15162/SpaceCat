      Welcome to SpaceCat!!

Get the highest score testing your skills troughout the game. Browse through space collecting as much food as possible but watch out for obstacles, they can cause harm.

Objective: The game consists of traveling through space collecting food. The player can move Up and Down and left to right using the the arrow keys.

Food

  • GoodFood This consists of Donuts, Strawberries, Sodas, pallets. It will get live or points depending on the type of food. Sodas and palettes give points. Donuts and strawberries give life.

  • BadFood This consists of Broccoli, Green Onion, Brussel Sprout. This takes away life or points depending on the type. Green Onion y Brussel Sprout remove points. The Broccoli remove live.

Enemy: The enemy is moving randomly moving in the middle of the screen attacking the player. If the golden food is obtained, the player could defeat him.

Obstacles: The player will have to go by avoiding them to collect as much food as possible.

  • Asteroid This obstacle takes away life and points.

  • SpaceWorm This obstacle takes away life and points. More points than the previous obstacle.
